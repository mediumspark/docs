---

QuickDraft requires no installation and no account to get started. Open a browser tab, navigate to the editor, and begin writing immediately. When your draft is ready, share it with a single link — collaborators can join and edit in real time without any sign-up.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open QuickDraft">
    Navigate to [https://www.quickdraft.in](https://www.quickdraft.in). The editor loads immediately in your browser, blank and ready to type — no loading screens, no onboarding flows.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start writing">
    Click into the editor and start typing. Use the toolbar at the top to apply formatting:

    - **Headings** — H1, H2, and H3 for structure
    - **Inline styles** — Bold, Italic, Underline, and Strikethrough
    - **Links** — Insert clickable hyperlinks
    - **Lists** — Ordered (numbered) and unordered (bullet) lists

    Your draft auto-saves to your browser's local storage as you type. You never need to press a save button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share your draft">
    Click **Copy Edit Link** to copy a shareable URL to your clipboard. Anyone with that link can view and edit the draft in real time.

    To share without granting editing rights, click **Copy Read-Only Link** instead. Recipients can read the draft but cannot make changes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save to your account (optional)">
    Click **Save to My Account** (the cloud icon ☁) to sync your draft to the cloud and access it from any device. You'll need to create a free account first — see the [Account sign-up page](/account/sign-up) for instructions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Bookmark your draft's URL or set a custom URL slug right away. QuickDraft drafts live at unique URLs — saving or customizing the link ensures you can find your draft again later.
</Tip>
