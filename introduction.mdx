QuickDraft is a distraction-free, browser-based writing space built for anyone who needs to capture ideas, draft content, or collaborate — without the friction of creating an account. Open your browser, start typing, and your work saves automatically. When you're ready to share, a single link lets collaborators jump in and edit alongside you in real time.
